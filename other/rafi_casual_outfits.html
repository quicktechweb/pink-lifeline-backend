GOLDEN RULES FOR YOUR FRAME

01

Fit first

Clothes should follow your body shape without being tight.

02

Half tuck

Tuck just the front of your shirt to define the waistline.

03

Roll sleeves

Show forearm skin to look more relaxed and capable.

04

One clean shoe

A clean shoe anchors even the most casual outfit.

05

Posture

The best garment looks poor on a slouched frame.
