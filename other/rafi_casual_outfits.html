Golden rules for your frame
Fit first — slim or straight fit only. Baggy kills every outfit.
Half tuck — tuck shirt halfway in. Looks intentional on slim guys.
Roll sleeves — mid-forearm. You already do this, keep it.
One clean shoe — dirty shoes ruin any outfit.
